Easy Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic staple that everyone should know for a quick and delicious dinner. Although it seems daunting, it is actually very easy to make and perfect for family dinners or meal prepping.

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and of course, lots of mozzarella cheese. Follow the recipe below to make it yourself!

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzeralla cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste

Lasagna Noodles/Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

Prep Work

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside.

Make the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil and add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 min.
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage. Cook for 8-10 min, then add garlic.
  3. Add the marinary sauce and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Begin boiling a large pot of salted pasta water. Once a rapid boil is reached, cook the noodles to al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water until noodles are completely cool.
  3. Lay the cooked noodles flat on parchment paper while you begin to assemble.

Assemble and Bake

  1. Spread 1 heading up of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13 inch casserole dish. Add 4 lasagna noodles, overlapping them if needed.
  2. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles followed by 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce.
  3. Top with 4 more lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese, and 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  4. Do this once more.
  5. Top with 2 1/2 cups of Mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake for 35 min. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
